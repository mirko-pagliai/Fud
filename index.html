FUD stands for Fuck U D... (guess what D means?). It was written and it is actively developed in order to offer a free software alternative to proprietary software just like Dropbox, Box.Net, etc. Users will be able to backup and/or to sync local folders and files to remote one via ssh, ftp or others protocols. The only thing needed is a ssh or ftp access on a server, even on a shared host. Enjoy!

Dependencies

curlftpfs inotify-tools libnotify-bin sshfs unison
